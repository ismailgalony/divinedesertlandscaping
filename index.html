About Us

Welcome to Divine Desert Landscaping, where we transform your outdoor spaces into breathtaking landscapes. With almost a decade of experience, our team of experts is dedicated to providing premium quality landscaping at budget-friendly rates. We offer free consulting to help you visualize and plan your dream outdoor space. From traditional landscape design to modern hardscaping, we bring your vision to life.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of professional landscaping services to transform your outdoor spaces. Here’s what we specialize in:

  • Landscape Design and Installation
  • Hardscaping (Pavers, Artificial Turf, Retaining Walls, Stone Pathways)
  • Irrigation Design and Repair
  • Outdoor Lighting
  • Maintenance and Cleanups
  • Tree Service and Planting
